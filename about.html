About Supertech Laminates :

Supertech Laminates Pvt. Ltd. is a quality name in the field of High Pressure Decorative Laminates providing the finest range and quality of decorative laminates at the least of cost. Driven by a passionate commitment to excellence and supported by the service of the best men and machine, our products are a preferred choice not just in India but also among our valued customers in Asia and across.

Our History

Supertech Laminates Pvt. Ltd. was founded in 2002 with a vision to provide best value for our customer's money. Spread in a sprawling 45K sq. ft., the manufacturing plant houses state of the art fully automatic machines that deliver a production of 30 lakh meter square laminate sheets per month. Operating in the Delhi-NCR region and well connected to the rest of the world, the unit is thus excellently equipped to cater bulk production targets efficiently. 

Our Philosophy:

Here in Supertech, we live by the words of our founder, Late Sh. Ramanand Gupta, who summarized the vision and core principles for the company in this one line

Put yourselves in place of the customer of every laminate manufactured here, and if there is any complaint your can foresee: Start Over!

His words continue to guide us and inform all aspects of our business.
